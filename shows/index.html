Our Shows!

There are a variety of shows that are aired everyday on Runway Radio to cater to its diverse pool of listeners. Students from all majors use this mutual space to talk about their capstones and final year projects, interesting study-abroad or UGrad experiences, startups and entrepreneurial endeavors, and to rant about the struggles of being an undergraduate student. Shows like On the Line, No Filter, Khilaadi No. 1, and As U Like It have been curated to meet different themes and interests for those in need of a filler whilst they go about their day at Habib University.
